# Importing worlds

By default, PNX utilizes `leveldb` to store world data.
If you plan to migrate to PNX and want to keep your worlds, follow the instructions below.

<Tabs items={['LevelDB', 'Other']}>
        <Tabs.Tab label="LevelDB">
            Follow these steps if your world is already using `leveldb`.
            <Steps>
                ### Copy into `worlds`
                Copy your world into the `worlds` folder of your server.

                ### Add configuration
                Create a suitable <a href="/worlds/config">config file</a> for your world.

                ### Reboot the server
                The world will be loaded on startup.
            </Steps>
        </Tabs.Tab>
        <Tabs.Tab label="Other">
            Follow these steps if your world is not using `leveldb` yet.
            <Steps>
                ### Convert the world
                Use a converter capable of converting your world format into `leveldb`.
                We recommend using [Chunker](https://chunker.app/)

                ### Copy into `worlds`
                Copy your converted world into the `worlds` folder of your server.

                ### Add configuration
                Create a suitable <a href="/worlds/config">config file</a> for your world.

                ### Reboot the server
                The world will be loaded on startup.
            </Steps>
        </Tabs.Tab>
    </Tabs>
